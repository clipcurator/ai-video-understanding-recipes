## Scoring

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Context clarity | Confusing | Understandable with setup | Clear without extra context |
| Emotional intensity | Flat | Noticeable | Strong |
| Visual interest | Static | Some movement | Visually strong |
| Narrative importance | Optional | Useful | Essential |
| Short-form fit | Hard to cut | Needs rewrite | Clip-ready |

## Notes

- Keep summaries factual.
- Separate visual cues from dialogue cues.
- Mark scenes that require context before they can work as standalone clips.
- Flag continuity risks before moving into editing.
